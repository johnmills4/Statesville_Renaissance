How to Donate to the Statesville High School Renaissance Foundation

If you wish to donate money or time to the Statesville High School Renaissance Foundation contact Mary Mills at marymills@bellsouth.net.

Donations can be delivered at Statesville High School main office to Mrs. Massenburg and checks can be made out to SHS Renaissance.

Why Donate to the Statesville High School Renaissance Foundation?

Your donation to the Renaissance Foundation allow us to fund lunches, purchase apparel and award students for academic excellence.
