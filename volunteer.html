Why Volunteer?

The Statesville High School Renaissance Foundation is a nonprofit school based program, and we proudly claim to be the only program of this kind in the county! As a volunteer you are able to both support the staff and reward students for academic excellence and good behavior. It is also a great way to be involved with your local high school, its students, and its teachers!
View img

How to get involved?

Here are some ways that you can make an impact through the Statesville High School Renaissance Foundation:

  • Provide baked goods and other food for teacher appreciation lunches.
  • Help sell apparel and concessions at the home football games.
  • Help with the final event of the year, the SHS Rock Concert.
  • Help give out t-shirts and goodie bags to all students who make renaissance.
  • And of course donations are always welcome!
