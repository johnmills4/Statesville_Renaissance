How to get involved with the Statesville High School Renaissance Foundation

    1. Provide baked goods and other food for teacher appreciation lunches.

    2. You can help sell apparel at the home football games.

    3. Help with the final event of the year which is the SHS Rock Concert. T-shirts will be given to all students who make renaissance.

    4. And of course donations are always welcome.

    Why you should volunteer for the Renaissance Foundation

    The Statesville High School Renaissance Foundation is a nonprofit school based program. Statesville High Schools is the only school in Iredell County with this organization. As a volunteer you are able to support the staff, reward students for academic excellence, and good behavior. It is also a great way to be involved with your high school student and get to know the teachers.
