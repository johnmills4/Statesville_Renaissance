Concessions

The concessions stand for athletic events is run by volunteers with the proceeds going to the athletic booster program. Renaissance volunteers several times a season in order to assist the boosters club.

teacher lunches

Twice a month, the members of Renaissance prepare lunch for the teachers. We feel that it is important to show our teachers appreciation and hopefully small gestures such as lunch can go a long way in that effort.
Get involved! Join our listserv to find out how you can help with the lunch preparations.

After each semester, students who qualify for Renaissance receive a gift bag with assorted goodies. These treats are distributed to students during homeroom block.

t-shirt design

Each year, Renaissance hosts a t-shirt design competition where students may submit their own designs. A winner is selected from the submitted design, and then featured as the yearly t-shirt that all renaissance students receive at the end of the year.
Have a design? Submit it by sending an email to marymills@bellsouth.net with the design attached.
